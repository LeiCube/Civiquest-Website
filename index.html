What is CiviQuest?

We need your help to investigate!

CiviQuest is an interactive story game where you play as a young journalist in Veritas City. Your mission is to investigate scandals, check suspicious posts, and expose the truth using fact-checking tools and clues you uncover along the way.

Mayor’s Statement Under Fact-Check

A viral video claims that Mayor Navarro made a controversial statement about shutting down community groups in Veritas. The clip is spreading fast across social media—fueling outrage, confusion, and heated debates.

FACT-CHECK NEWS

Viral Footage Still Under Review

The Veritas Post is currently examining the viral video of Mayor Navarro that has been circulating online. The team is carefully reviewing the footage to confirm whether it is authentic or manipulated. Until verification is complete, they urge the public to remain cautious and avoid spreading unverified claims.
